Ven is a Master of Commerce candidate at the University of New South Wales, majoring in Finance and Economics.

He has a strong foundation in analytical finance with a growing interest in investment banking, corporate finance, and transaction advisory. His internship experience includes financial analysis, cost modeling, and creating pitch materials, giving him practical exposure to profitability analysis, reporting frameworks, and pricing strategy.

Ven actively builds and maintains a personal portfolio of valuation models—DCF, comps, and LBOs—and develops Python-based tools for automation and financial research.

Based in Sydney, with academic exposure across India and Australia, Ven brings a global perspective, analytical discipline, and a strong drive to build a long-term career in finance.
